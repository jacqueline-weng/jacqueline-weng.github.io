To Stop Thinking is Spiritual Death

Jacqueline Weng | Thu 23 April 2020

Discussion about Detachment

To stop thinking is spiritual death. If one stops criticizing and reflecting himself, he is likely to sink in the deep water of negative emotions and the tragedy of life. When a rational person reflects on himself, often the case he will detach himself from the current occation.The critiques and analysis under detachment is believed to be rational, away from the effect brought by personal emotions. For example, if one is drown in great anger, he tends to be defensive to others in order to protect himself. He inclines to find excuses from the outer environment.However, if he tries to detach himself from his emotions, he is no longer under the control of strong and negative emotions. He can see the problem more rational and comes up with solutions that can get himself out of the situation. Therefore, a principle of living a life with high efficiency is to keep thinking rationally. Life is to solve unlimited number of problems. If you are wasting time in the nagative emotions, it is obviously not helpful to solve problems.
